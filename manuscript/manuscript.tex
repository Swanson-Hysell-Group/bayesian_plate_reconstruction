the past 200 Ma record is relatively well understood, based on the oceanic crust's magnetic anomalies 

Beyond then, the oceanic record is gone due to the subduction of the oceanic lithosphere near plate boundaries, and our record is dominated by the paleomagnetic records from continental rocks, which usually consist of a pole with uncertainty ellipse and an associated age constraints.
